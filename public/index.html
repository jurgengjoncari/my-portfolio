About Me

My name is Jurgen Gjonçari, and I'm a web developer with experience in Angular, Loopback, NodeJS, JavaScript, MongoDB, and more. Although I studied Mathematics at Luigj Gurakuqi University of Shkodra, I decided to pursue a career in web development in 2022.

As a developer, I'm a perfectionist and like to think out of the box to find the best solutions. I'm always eager to learn and explore new technologies to improve my skills.

In my free time, I enjoy painting and reading about new technologies, as well as exploring the deeper meanings of the world around us.
